What Is the Vagus Nerve?

The vagus nerve is the longest cranial nerve in the body, running from the brainstem through the neck, chest and abdomen. It is the primary highway of the parasympathetic nervous system. When the vagus nerve is well-toned, you are calm, digesting well and healing efficiently. When under-active, you are chronically stressed, inflamed and prone to illness. Vagal tone is one of the most important — and most overlooked — markers of overall health.

How OM Activates the Vagus Nerve

This is perhaps the most scientifically validated mechanism in AVM Academy's methodology. The vagus nerve innervates the larynx and pharynx — the voice box and throat. When you chant OM, the vibration directly stimulates vagal branches in the throat, sending parasympathetic signals throughout the body simultaneously. This is why OM produces such immediate feelings of calm, connection and physical wellbeing.

The AVM Vagal Activation Protocol

Diaphragmatic Breathing: The diaphragm massages vagal branches with every deep breath, stimulating parasympathetic activation.

Extended Exhale OM: Make your OM exhale as long as possible — 15 to 20 seconds. The extended exhale is the single most powerful vagal activation technique available.

Continuous Humming: Between OM rounds, simply hum. Humming vibrates vagal branches in the throat continuously, creating sustained parasympathetic activation.

Signs of Improving Vagal Tone

Resting heart rate variability (HRV) increases — measurable with modern smartwatches. Digestion improves. Inflammatory conditions reduce. Anxiety decreases. Social connection feels easier and more natural. Recovery from stress happens progressively faster. These are all signatures of a well-toned vagus nerve.

Why This Is the Most Important Practice

If AVM Academy had to identify one practice with the broadest systemic health benefits, vagal activation through OM would be it. It simultaneously improves cardiovascular health, mental health, digestive function, immune response and social bonding capacity. It is, quite literally, the master switch for human wellbeing.
